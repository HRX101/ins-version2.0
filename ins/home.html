ECSF

Estuarine and Coastal Studies Foundation (ECSF) a coastal-marine research and higher education organization. The founders of ECSF are scientists, administrators, and social workers with various capacities in the society. ECSF was established on 1 October 2021 as a non-profit organisation (section 8 of Companies Act 2013) at Howrah, West Bengal,India with the aim

  • To undertake and facilitate research and development to attain sustainability in estuarine, coastal and marine environment
  • To undertake and facilitate education on estuarine, coastal and marine environment and train manpower for research and development on such fields
  • To undertake, facilitate and address Sustainable Development Goals of United Nation
  • To undertake, facilitate and advocate sustainable and decentralized natural resource management practises as the principal means of building coastal societies and foster the use of local and indigenous knowledge as a key of conserving estuarine, coastal and marine environment.
